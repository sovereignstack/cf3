# Privacy Policy — Tread

_Last updated: 2026_

Tread is built so that protecting your data isn't a promise we ask you to trust — it's how the app physically works. This policy explains, in plain language, what that means.

## The short version

- Tread has **no account, no server, and no backend**.
- Everything you enter is stored **only in your browser, on your device**.
- Nothing is transmitted, sold, shared, profiled, or used for advertising.
- There are **no analytics and no third-party trackers**.
- You can **export** or **permanently erase** all of your data at any time, instantly, from the *You* tab.

## What data Tread stores

Only what's needed to calculate and show your footprint:

- Your setup answers (travel, diet, home energy, household size, shopping habits).
- Your activity logs (what you logged, the amount, the date, and the calculated CO₂e).
- Your pledges, badges, points, and streak.
- Your settings (text size, high contrast, reduced motion, region).
- A record that you gave consent, with the version and timestamp.

Tread does **not** ask for or store your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or any payment or banking information.

## Where your data lives

In your browser's local storage, on the device you're using. It does not travel to us or to anyone else, because there is nowhere for it to travel — Tread operates no servers and makes no network requests for your data. The only network activity the app performs is the service worker caching the app's *own* files (the HTML, icons, and manifest) so the app works offline. No personal data is involved in that.

## Why Tread processes it (purpose)

Solely to calculate and display your carbon footprint, trends, and personalised suggestions on your device. That's the only purpose.

## Legal basis

Your **consent**, given through a clear affirmative action (an unticked checkbox you choose to tick) before any data is entered — consistent with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, and Article 6(1)(a) of the EU GDPR. Consent is not bundled with anything else, because there is no optional or secondary processing to opt into.

## Your rights

Because your data is local and under your direct control, these are immediate:

- **Access** — see everything stored via *View what's stored* in the *You* tab.
- **Portability** — download all your data as a JSON file via *Export my data*.
- **Correction** — edit or delete any individual log, or redo your setup, at any time.
- **Erasure** — *Delete everything* permanently removes all local data after a clear confirmation.
- **Withdraw consent** — one tap in the *You* tab, as easy as giving it, with immediate erasure.

If Tread were ever operated as a hosted service by an organisation, you would also have the right to raise a grievance with that operator and to complain to the relevant authority (in India, the Data Protection Board). As a local-only tool, there is no operator holding your data.

## Retention

Your data stays until you delete it or clear your browser storage. There is no server-side retention, because there is no server.

## Children

Tread is general-audience, requests no identifying information, and sends nothing off the device. It is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any personal identifiers from anyone.

## Security

Your data remains within your browser's sandboxed storage on your device. As with anything stored locally, its security also depends on the security of your device and browser. The JSON export is provided so you can keep your own backups.

## Changes

Because Tread runs entirely on your device with no server, any change to how it handles data would arrive as an update to the app itself, and the in-app notice and this document would be updated accordingly.

## Contact

Tread is a personal, local-first tool with no operator backend. For questions about a specific deployment of Tread, contact the person or team who deployed it.
